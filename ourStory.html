GPS Renewables : The Journey

GPS Renewables was founded with a vision to revolutionize the biofuels industry by transforming waste into clean energy. What started as a single project has grown into a leading force in sustainable energy, with advanced biofuel solutions deployed across India and beyond.


Today, we specialize in Renewable Natural Gas (RNG)/Compressed Biogas (CBG), 2G Ethanol and Sustainable Aviation Fuel (SAF), working towards a future where clean energy powers industries and communities sustainably.

Vision
To lead the global transition towards a sustainable future powered by innovative clean energy solutions.

Mission
To pioneer developing and implementing cutting-edge clean fuel technologies, driving the shift towards renewable energy sources.

Values
Innovation, sustainability, Technology, Integrity, Collaboration.
