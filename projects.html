Feedstock: Paddy Straw, Napier Grass, PressMud Cake, Mustard Trash and Cotton Stalk

Key Projects: Barabanki (Uttar Pradesh), Jhajjar (Haryana), Panipat (Haryana) and rest all add from sheets

Processing organic waste from cities to produce biofuels:

Feedstock: Organic fraction of MSW

Key Projects: Bhopal (Madhya Pradesh), Indore (Madhya Pradesh)

MSW pre-treatment involves sorting and separating organic fractions from non-organic materials before further processing into biogas or biofuels:

Feedstock: MSW

Key Projects: Vadodara (Gujarat), Prayagraj (Uttar Pradesh)
