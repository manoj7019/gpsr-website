FAQs

  • GPS Renewables is a leading clean-tech company focused on sustainable waste-to-energy solutions. We specialize in BioCNG and biogas technologies that transform organic waste into renewable energy.

  • BioCNG (Compressed Biogas) is a renewable fuel derived from organic waste. It serves as a clean alternative to fossil fuels, reducing greenhouse gas emissions and promoting energy security.

  • Our business model includes turnkey project development, technology licensing, and O&M services. We also operate select BioCNG plants and partner with stakeholders in public and private sectors.

  • We serve sectors including waste management, oil & gas, food processing, hospitality, agriculture, and public utilities. Our solutions are scalable for municipal corporations, industries, and institutions.

  • GPS Renewables has commissioned and is executing several BioCNG and biogas plants across India. Our portfolio includes high-impact projects with municipal corporations, large industries, and strategic partners.

  • Our proprietary technologies offer higher efficiency, modular scalability, lower carbon footprint, and faster payback periods. We are constantly innovating through R&D and pilot testing.

  • Yes, we’ve been recognized by national and international bodies for innovation and impact, including the Ministry of New and Renewable Energy (MNRE), UNDP, and CII.

  • We collaborate with government agencies, oil marketing companies (OMCs), waste management firms, and industrial clients. Our partnerships are strategic for scale and impact.

  • We aim to scale our BioCNG infrastructure across India, expand into international markets, invest in advanced technology R&D, and pursue inorganic growth opportunities.

  • Our revenue streams include EPC contracts, technology licensing, O&M services, plant operations, and carbon credit monetization.

  • India’s BioCNG sector is poised for exponential growth driven by government initiatives like SATAT, increasing waste generation, and demand for clean transport fuels.

  • As of now, GPS Renewables is a privately held company. We continue to evaluate strategic funding options to fuel our growth.

  • Currently, we work with institutional investors and strategic partners. For investment inquiries, please reach out via our Contact or Investor Relations page.

  • GPS Renewables was founded by IIM alumni with deep expertise in clean-tech. The leadership team brings decades of experience in renewable energy, engineering, and project development.

  • We are committed to climate action, circular economy practices, and inclusive growth. Our operations align with global ESG and SDG frameworks.

  • You can find our latest news, project highlights, and investor-related announcements on the Investor Relations section of our website.
